# eFootball Pull Tracker

Track your eFootball spins, analyze your luck, and know exactly what you've pulled.

## Features

- **User Accounts** — Register and login securely
- **eFootball Profile** — Store your in-game username, platform, and account creation date
- **Pull Logging** — Record every player you pull (name, rating, position, card type, pack, coins spent)
- **Full History** — View, filter, sort, and manage your pull history
- **Detailed Analytics** — Charts for rating distribution, card types, positions, and monthly activity
- **Statistics Dashboard** — Total spins, best pull, legendary pulls count, account age
